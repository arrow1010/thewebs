More about us

Kids On Mission is a karate school which was established by sensei Kabelo Ogomoditse Molefe in 2009 at tlokweng and 2010 it moved to gaborone in block6 superfit gym,when we started our aim was to work with kids so that they can join us in our mission of going around motivating kids who are not active in sports. So far we've produced good and promising athletes who are going to be future champions,the most important or interesting part about us is we're not only focused in karate we're also dealing with fitness and other sporting activities.
